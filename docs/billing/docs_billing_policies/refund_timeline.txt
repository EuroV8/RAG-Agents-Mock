Refund Policy Breakdown

Starter plan policy:
- Refunds are eligible during the first thirty days of the billing cycle.
- After thirty days, offer prorated credit if uptime drops below ninety-nine percent.

Growth plan policy:
- Provide a full refund within thirty days when onboarding blockers are documented.
- After thirty days, handle each request case by case and resolve within thirty days of submission.

Scale plan policy:
- Terms follow the master services agreement.
- Issue credits when service-level agreements are missed for two consecutive weeks.
- Early termination requires sixty days written notice.

Supporting artifacts:
- Maintain audit logs of customer communication in the CRM.
- Attach incident identifiers whenever SLA violations are involved.
