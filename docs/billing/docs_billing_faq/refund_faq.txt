Billing Refund FAQ

Submitting a refund request:
- Direct customers to the self-service form at https://inksupport.example.com/refunds.
- Remind them to include their workspace ID, invoice number, and a short issue summary.

Initial response time:
- Billing acknowledges submissions within one business day.
- Cases escalate to the Billing Agent whenever automated checks detect missing information.

Approval guidelines:
- Starter and Growth plans require proof that adoption blockers were reported within thirty days of billing.
- Scale plan refunds follow the master services agreement and need executive approval.

Payout process:
- Approved refunds are processed in the nightly batch and typically arrive within five business days.
- Customers receive an email confirmation that includes a tracking ID and expected payout timeline.
